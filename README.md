## Research Interests
- Nuclear astrophysics and r-process nucleosynthesis

- Experimental nuclear astrophysics and detector-based observations

##  Research Projects
### Python-Based Monte Carlo Nuclear Reaction Network for the Iron Isotopic Chain
**Tools:** Python, TALYS, KADoNiS, NumPy, SciPy  

- Developed a neutron-capture reaction network for the 56–60Fe isotopic chain  
- Integrated TALYS-generated cross sections and MACS calculations  
- Compared results with KADoNiS data and analyzed uncertainties  
- Produced isotope evolution and reaction-flow visualizations  

### Krypton Isotope Production in 15 M☉ Stars
**Status:** Research article under review  
**Tools:** MESA, TALYS, Python  

- Implemented TALYS-based reaction rates into MESA  
- Studied the impact of metallicity and helium mass fraction on Kr isotope yields  
- Analyzed reaction rates, abundances, and sensitivity trends  

### r-Process Heating and β-Delayed Neutron Uncertainties
**Tools:** TALYS, SkyNet, Python  

- Investigated the impact of β-delayed neutron emission uncertainties on r-process heating  
- Studied consequences for kilonova-relevant abundance signatures
  
### Radio Astronomy Data Analysis (TART)
**Tools:** Python, TART, interferometric imaging  

- Performed telescope calibration and visibility measurements  
- Reconstructed interferometric sky images from real observational data  
- Gained experience handling noisy experimental datasets
